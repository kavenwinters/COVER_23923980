SPORTS I GREW UP PLAYING BASEBALL AND BASKETBALL, AND HAVE CONTINUED PLAYING THE INTRAMURALS THAT THE U OF A HAS TO OFFER. I AM ALWAYS SURPRISED BY THE INFINITE LESSONS YOU CAN LEARN THROUGH COMPETITION.
