BACKSTORY

Coming from a small town to the University of Arizona was a big but needed change for me. To really put it into perspective, there were more residents in my dorm last year than citizens in my hometown. With such a change came looming decisions: what was I to do with my time? How would I prioritize school and social life when everything here operated so differently? The solution was what led me to the U of A in the first place. Go where the opportunity takes me. I've stuck with it so far, and it has allowed me to connect with so many new people and explore their unique perspectives on life, and I can honestly say I can't imagine myself anywhere else.
